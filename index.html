NotebookLM Sources Hub

Curated directory of websites hosting free files & knowledge for Google NotebookLM sources. Download PDFs, ebooks, papers, datasets — upload to your notebooks.

How to use: Download files from any site below → open NotebookLM → click "Add source" → upload PDF/text/audio, or paste URL. Limit: 50 sources/notebook (300 for Plus). Max 500KB text, 200MB per source.
